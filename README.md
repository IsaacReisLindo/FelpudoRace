                                                       Game description
In the game, you control Felpudo, who is once again in search of Fofura, who has been kidnapped by Uruca. The goal of the game is to reach 1,500 points by killing 15 enemies (each one provides 100 points)
while avoiding being hit by them, since when you are hit many times your life reaches zero and you lose the game. Gameplay Image <img width="1829" height="973" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/72de8c87-b98a-40e0-bbdf-c02b65d37e65" />

                                                       Controls
In the game, you use the right mouse button to shoot, the space bar or up arrow to jump, and the A and D keys or arrow keys to move left and right.

                                                       Compatibility
The game is only compatible with computers.
